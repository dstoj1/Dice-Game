Dice Math

Roll the dice. (Click the Die to Start.)

Choose if you would like your child to practice Adding, Subtraction, Multiplication or Division.

Then have them roll the dice. Whatever numbers rolled your child will either Add, Subtract, Multiply or Divide the numbers.

When your child gets 3 correct answers they will move to the next level. If all the levels are completed they WIN!!!! Once they have completed the section they were working on they can move to another section. GOOD LUCK!
